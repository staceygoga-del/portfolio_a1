What is the Sims 4 ?

The Sims 4 is the highly anticipated life simulation game that lets you play with life like never before. Create new Sims with intelligence and emotion.It was created by the studio Maxis and the publisher EA (Electronic Arts) which is responsible for distributing the game worldwide.
