Why play the Sims 4?

"In The Sims, you control the story. Or let the story control you!" – EA Games

The Sims 4 is a life simulation game where you can create unique Sims, build homes, and control their lives. Here are some of the best features:

  • Build Mode
  • Life Simulation
  • Careers
  • Create-A-Sim
  • Open Worlds

🏡 Build Mode – Create Your Dream Home

  • Drag-and-drop building tools for simple house creation.
  • Customize furniture, wallpapers, and flooring.
  • Use the Gallery to download and share home designs.

Back to Top

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Life Simulation – Control Every Moment

Experience realistic Sims emotions, relationships, and careers.

  • Build relationships, get married, and start families.
  • Experience emotions that affect gameplay and interactions.
  • Follow different life paths from childhood to elder years.

Back to Top

💼 Careers & Aspirations – Follow Your Dreams

Choose from a variety of career paths to help your Sims succeed.

  • Pick from careers like Doctor, Astronaut, Chef, or Freelancer.
  • Climb the career ladder and earn promotions.
  • Complete work-from-home tasks for freelance careers.

Back to Top

🎭 Create-A-Sim – Express Your Style

Customize your Sims' look, personality, and fashion.

  • Change facial features, hairstyles, and clothing.
  • Choose personality traits and aspirations.
  • Mix and match accessories, tattoos, and more!

Back to Top

🌍 Open Worlds – Explore Different Neighborhoods

Visit exciting locations and meet unique Sims.

  • Travel between different neighborhoods seamlessly.
  • Discover unique locations like parks, restaurants, and beaches.
  • Meet Sims with different cultures and backgrounds.

Back to Top

🎮 Expansion Packs – Enhance Your Gameplay

The Sims 4 offers a variety of expansion packs that add new features. Check out the Expansion Packs page for more!
