What Are Expansion Packs?

Expansion packs in The Sims 4 adds new worlds, careers, lifestyles, and gameplay features to enhance the player experience. Each pack brings unique content to explore.

All The Sims 4 Expansion Packs

The Sims 4: Seasons

Seasons brings dynamic weather, seasonal holidays, and an impact on Sims' daily lives.

The Sims 4: City Living

Experience apartment life, festivals, and careers in the city of San Myshuno.

The Sims 4: Island Living

Live on a tropical island, meet mermaids, and work as a conservationist.

The Sims 4: Cats & Dogs

Adopt pets, train them, and open your own veterinary clinic.

The Sims 4:Disover University

Send your Sims to university, live in dorms, and work toward a degree.

The Sims 4:Get Together

Join clubs, dance at nightclubs, and explore the European-inspired world of Windenburg.

The Sims 4:Get to Work

Control Sims in active careers like detective, doctor, and scientist.

The Sims 4:Get Famous

Become a celebrity, earn fame points, and live in luxury.

The Sims 4:Eco lifestyle

Go green, reduce pollution, and live an eco-friendly lifestyle.

The Sims 4:Horse Ranch

Own a horse ranch, raise horses, and compete in equestrian events.

The Sims 4:Growing together

Enhance family gameplay with new relationship dynamics and milestones.

The Sims 4:Cottage Living

Live in the countryside, raise farm animals, and grow your own food.

The Sims 4:Parenthood

Shape your Sims' childhoods and parenting styles.

The Sims 4:Paranormal

Experience haunted houses, summon spirits, and become a paranormal investigator.
